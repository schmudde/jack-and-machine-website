JACK and the MACHINE

The War Years

Jack Tramiel entered the world just as the power of automated information processing became widely understood. Punch card machines made the administration of ambitious centrally-administered social programs possible.

In the United States, they were used for programs such as Social Security. In Nazi Germany, these same machines would be used for more nefarious purposes.
